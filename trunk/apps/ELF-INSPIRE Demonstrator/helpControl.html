Map navigation

  • Click and drag to pan the map.
  • Double click on the map to zoom in on that spot.
  • Press and hold Shift to drag a rectangle to zoom in on.
  • Use the mouse scroll weel to zoom in or out, or use the slider in the top left of the map.

Downloading data

  1. Hold the Alt key and drag a rectangle to define the download area (your area of interest).
  2. Select one or more feature types in the Map layers panel.
  3. Click the Download Features button. Matching Download Services will be queried. From each of these services you will receive a GML file with geographical features.

Searching by name

To search for a geographical name, start typing the name in the search field above the map. After four characters have been entered, a search for matching names will take place. The search is refined when more characters are typed. Some matching names are denoted by a (EuroGeoNames). This means that the name comes from EuroGeoNames. After a single name has been selected (or typed completely), the map will be zoomed in on the location of the named place identified by the name.
