This is the prototype web demonstrator of the ESDIN project intended for use by the ESDIN consortium and EuroGeographics members only. It allows views and downloads of official data. It is not intended as a browser based map viewer to rival Google Maps. It is here to show how INSPIRE services can work and enable interoperation. The data are provided by the various National Mapping and Cadastral Agencies that participate in ESDIN.

Data for searching by name are provided by EuroGeoNames and GeoNames.org.

For discussion on this viewer please use EuroGeoForum.
